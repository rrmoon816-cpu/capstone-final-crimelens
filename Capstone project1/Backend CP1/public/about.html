About CrimeLens

CrimeLens: A Crime Management Portal is a public awareness platform created to help citizens reach the correct and official government websites related to crime, safety, cyber awareness, and emergency services.

Our Mission

To provide a single, easy-to-access platform for all crime-related government resources in India.

Disclaimer

This is NOT an official government website.

We do NOT collect FIRs or legal complaints.

We only guide users to authentic and verified government portals.

Why This Platform?

  • People are confused about correct official links.
  • Fake websites misguide citizens — our portal prevents that.
  • One place for all central and state-level crime resources.
